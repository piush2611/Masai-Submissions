We have a well-defined admission process so that we only get the highly motivated and deserving students in the course. It is defined step by step as follows:
Phase I Apply on the website: https://admissions.masaischool.com and complete the application process which consists of two parts. Every part has a well-thought objective to be assessed.
Phase II Once you complete the above step, we will evaluate your application and if you scored above the cut off score, you sign an admission letter for joining the Pre-course (1 week duration). Pre-course is a mandatory course for all the students.
Phase III If you qualify the above Phase II, then you will get a call from the admissions team at Masai School for a 15 minutes face-to-face or Skype interview.

In our entire admission process, we are only looking for one thing: motivation to learn and earn. Our course is very intensive and demanding, and we want the most motivated people to get onboard. Make sure that you explain why you want to join Masai School in your application - the more details you give us, the better chance you will have to get selected for the interview!

There are two payment options that a learner can choose from. Based on the option chosen, the amount paid by a learner will differ.
Option 1 - One-Time Fee Student pays at the beginning of the course, a fixed fee of ₹2 lakhs +GST. No additional amount needs to be paid after this at any stage of the program or after completion.
Option 2 - Income Share Agreement (ISA) The student signs an Income Share Agreement (ISA) with Masai School. No upfront payment needs to be made. You need to pay only after you get a job with a salary of ₹6 lakhs per annum (before taxes). The payment will be done in monthly installments @15% of the per month gross salary. The installments will continue till the 36th month or until you have paid us an amount of ₹3 lakhs + GST, whichever is earlier. The Income Share Agreement is capped at ₹3 lakhs + GST. In the rarest scenario where, you don't get a job or you get a job with a salary less than ₹6 lakhs/annum, we don’t charge you at all.

Yes, there is a preparatory work, the only technical prerequisite to join the course is to complete the Pre-Course of Masai School. It generally takes between 40- 50 hours, and it is mandatory. Once you are selected, to help you kickstart your journey into coding and make sure you start at the same level as everyone else, you will need to complete our Pre-course, before the beginning of the course.

  • If you drop out before the first 4-week mark, your ISA is cancelled.​​
  • If you drop out between week 4 and week 12 (course midway), your ISA duration gets prorated based on the completed weeks.
  • If you drop out after 12 weeks (course midway), you are completely liable to the entire duration of the ISA.

ISA is a legally binding contract between Masai School and you. It requires you to furnish your Income Tax Return documents (ITR) and other income proofs to Masai School.

Yes, as we are going to invest hugely on your learning we will need a security collateral from your end. For this purpose, you will have to give us two undated Bank Account Cheque of ₹ 1,00,000 each (You don't need to have this amount in your bank account, this is only for security purpose). This cheque will be returned to you after the agreement period. We won’t encash it but we will have the right to use this cheque in case of any breach of Income share agreement from your end.
